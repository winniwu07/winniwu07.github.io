Midori's Traveler's Notebook

The traveler's notebook is a widely used journaling system where the user is able to modify and customize any part of the journal, due to its modularity. The quality of their products are top tier while still being affordable.

Traveler's Company is a Japanese stationary brand, parent company Midori, that specialize in the leather notebook covers that have caused many other brands to follow. All in-person stores are located in Japan with limited items uniuque to the location available for purchase. Partner shops are also located all around world.
